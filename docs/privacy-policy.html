Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Pokemon Card Search ("the App") is a free, non-commercial application developed by KenAu. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login is required
  • No personal data (name, email, phone number) is collected
  • No location data is accessed
  • No camera or microphone access is used
  • No contacts or files are accessed
  • No advertising identifiers are collected

Network Usage

The App requires an internet connection solely to fetch Pokémon card data and pricing information from external APIs. These network requests do not transmit any personal or identifying information about the user.

Local Data Storage

The App may cache card data locally on your device to improve performance. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any third party. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's cache or uninstalling the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the app's GitHub repository.
